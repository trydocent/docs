Docent pricing is simple. Docent adds a fee on Hyperliquid perpetual trades, and no added Docent fee on spot or prediction market trades.

## At a glance

| Activity | Docent fee | What else may apply |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Hyperliquid perpetuals | `1 bps` of notional (`0.01%`) | Venue base fees |
| Hyperliquid spot | `0` | Venue base fees |
| Polymarket prediction markets | `0` | Venue base fees |

## What that looks like in practice

`1 bps` means `0.01%` of trade notional.

Examples:

- A `$10,000` perpetual trade adds a `$1.00` Docent fee
- A `$25,000` perpetual trade adds a `$2.50` Docent fee

If you use leverage, the fee still applies to trade notional, not only to your posted margin.

## What Docent does not charge for

Docent does not charge fees for:

- Deposits
- Withdrawals
- Sending USDC to another user
- Moving funds across supported chains or venues

Network fees, venue fees, or third-party provider fees may still apply.

## The simple rule

If you trade perpetuals, Docent adds `1 bps`.

If you trade spot or prediction markets, Docent adds no extra fee on top of the venue's base fees.
