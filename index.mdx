With Docent, you have direct access to [Hyperliquid](https://hyperfoundation.org/) perpetuals with up to `40x` leverage, spot tokens, and prediction markets on [Polymarket](https://polymarket.com/) at your fingertips. Docent is fully non-custodial and you stay in charge of your funds at all times.

Our wallet infrastructure is built on top of industry leaders [Privy](https://www.privy.io/) and [OneBalance](https://www.onebalance.io/).
