With Docent, you have direct access to Hyperliquid perpetuals with up to `40x` leverage, spot tokens, and prediction markets on Polymarket at your fingertips. Docent is fully non-custodial and you stay in charge of your funds at all times. Our wallet infrastructure is built on`Privy` and `OneBalance`.

<Note>
  Docent is currently on a waitlist. Join at [trydocent.com](https://trydocent.com).
</Note>

## Where your trade happens

Docent is the app layer. Your trade still happens on the venue you choose.

- `Hyperliquid` for perpetuals and spot
- `Polymarket` for prediction markets
