Docent is a mobile trading app for Hyperliquid and Polymarket. We built it for traders who want to move from funding to trade without juggling separate apps for each venue.

With Docent, you can trade Hyperliquid perpetuals with up to `40x` leverage, trade spot markets on Hyperliquid, and trade prediction markets on Polymarket from one app. Deposits on Base, Arbitrum, and Hyperliquid feed into a simpler balance view, while your wallet stays in your control through non-custodial infrastructure powered by `Privy` and `OneBalance`.

<Note>
  Docent is currently on a waitlist. Join at [trydocent.com](https://trydocent.com).
</Note>

## What you can do

- Trade Hyperliquid perpetuals
- Trade Hyperliquid spot markets
- Trade Polymarket prediction markets
- Deposit USDC on Base, Arbitrum, and Hyperliquid
- Use a simpler balance view across supported chains and venues
- Export your wallet key when you need it

## Where your trade happens

Docent is the app layer. Your trade still happens on the venue you choose.

- `Hyperliquid` for perpetuals and spot
- `Polymarket` for prediction markets

That gives you a simpler product experience without hiding where your order is executed.
