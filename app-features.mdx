Docent is built to help you move from idea to trade faster.

## Trade Hyperliquid perpetuals

Use Docent to trade Hyperliquid perpetuals with up to `40x` leverage.

Docent gives you a simpler mobile flow for opening, managing, and tracking positions while still trading on Hyperliquid.

## Trade Hyperliquid spot

Use the same app to buy and sell supported spot markets on Hyperliquid.

That keeps spot and perpetuals close together instead of splitting them across different products.

## Trade Polymarket prediction markets

Use Docent to browse and trade prediction markets on Polymarket.

You can move from market discovery to order entry without leaving the app.

## Fund your account without extra friction

You can deposit USDC on Base, Arbitrum, and Hyperliquid, then use that balance in the app without manually stitching together every step yourself.

Docent keeps the path from funding to trade simpler.

## Use one product across those venues

Docent brings discovery, funding, order entry, and balance viewing into one mobile experience.

You do not need to think in terms of disconnected apps just to move from one type of market to another.

## Keep control of your wallet

Docent is non-custodial. It uses `Privy` and `OneBalance` to make the wallet and balance experience easier to use, while still letting you export your wallet key when you need it.

## What comes next

Docent also has a dedicated `Docent` tab for guided actions. More detailed documentation on language model features is coming soon.
